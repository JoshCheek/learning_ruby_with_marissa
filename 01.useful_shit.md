* making sense of syntax: `ruby-parse -e '"omghi"' 2>/dev/null`
  "AST" = "Abstract Syntax Tree"
* Look at AST from Ruby:

  ```ruby
  require 'parser/ruby27'

  Parser::Ruby27.parse(<<RUBY)
    cat
  RUBY
  # => s(:send, nil, :cat)
  ```
